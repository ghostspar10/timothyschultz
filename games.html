The Mountain (Unity VR)

The Mountain is a VR game I worked on for my major to get practice with in Unity and VR development. Honestly, it was fun to work on, but I'm very not happy with it as there are just so many improvements that can be made to it. Once I can eventually afford a VR headset, I think I'll return back to update the game and see if I can improve it.

Kill The Virus (Adobe Flash)

Kill The Virus is an Adobe Animate game I worked on back in 2017, when I first started making games. It's the first game I ever made, and I was very happy with it. It featured no sound and had incredibly poor optimization which is really funny to look back on. I'd like to go back and expand upon it one day, maybe remake it in Unity rather than Animate since swf games are kind of dead now.

Grocery Grab (Unity VR)

Grocery Grab is a VR game intended to children who struggle with autism in the Boise School district learn how to go shopping without the hardships of going to the store in reality. This was a group project we picked up from our seniors in the GIMM program, and we made major strides in improving the look, performance, and feel of the whole project. We're quite happy with the current state of the project, even though there are still many improvements that can be made.
