Who Am I?

I'm a game developer and current senior at Boise State University studying under the GIMM (Gaming, Interactive Media and Mobile technology) program. I love games and I love game design, especially coding. Nothing is more satisfying for me than balancing games to be fun and writing efficient code. I hope to make many more games as I continue to learn and improve.


Check out my GitHub to see random projects I've accumulated throughout the years, or check out the Games tab to see what I've worked on so far!
